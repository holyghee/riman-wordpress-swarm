## Conclusion

The content mapping reveals a strong foundation of management and coordination expertise that can be leveraged for mediation services. While significant new content development is required for mediation-specific sections, the existing technical content provides excellent support for the construction safety management branch and establishes credibility for expanded mediation services.

The unique positioning of combining technical safety coordination with mediation services creates a distinctive market position that should be emphasized throughout the content strategy.
